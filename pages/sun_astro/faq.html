Sun Astro FAQ

1. What is Sun Astro?

Sun Astro is India’s best digital consultation and practice management app which has been built to cater to the needs of a modern astrologers. We have made it extremely easy to transition from a pen-paper state to a fully digitized. The application has been built differently for each speciality to help you be as familiar with the software as possible. Our advanced probability algorithms learn from every interaction and improves the app experience as you use it.

2. How does it work?

Sun Astro comprises of 2 apps:
Sun Astro - This module is accessible only to the astrologer and is used for consultation and maintenance of user’s appointment details and records.
Sun Astro for users – Users can login and book their appointment for video consultation and access their prediction document from the Sun Astro app which reduces their dependence on retaining a printed copy

3. Does Sun Astro require specialised hardware?

Sun Astro is currently available on the android platform. So if you wish you can keep a separate android smart phone or Tab for video consulting.

4. How safe is my data?

All Sun Astro servers are hosted within Virtual Private Servers, with multiple data backup security. This means that your data is only accessible to you, just like your bank account.
