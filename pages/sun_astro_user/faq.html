Sun Astro User FAQ

1. How do I book video consultation appointments?

Please select the type Astrology / Pooja and search for the Astrologer. After selecting the Astrologer, you can choose the preferred time slot and proceed to make the payment for booking your video consultation appointment.

2. I want to cancel my appointment?

Please go to the 'Appointments' section and choose the appointment you want to modify. Select the Cancel icon for cancelling the appointment.

3. I am not able to search my astrologer for appointment booking

Please search using astrologer’s full name and spelling it correctly. If you are still not able to find it, please write us at admin@magratechs.com and we will be glad to help you

4. I am getting 'No slots available' while booking the appointment

All the appointment slots for the chosen date are filled up. Please select a different date and a slot to book your appointment.

5. What can I use the video consultation option for?

You can use the option to get the consultation done in case it is not possible for you to visit the Astrologer in his office.

6. How can I video consult with the astrologer?

Please select the type Astrology / Pooja and search for the Astrologer. After selecting the Astrologer, you can choose the preferred time slot and proceed to make the payment for booking your video consultation appointment. The Astrologer will call you for the consultation at the scheduled time.

7. What if the call gets disconnected while consulting with the Astrologer?

In case the call gets disconnected in between the consultation, don’t worry, the astrologer will call you back shortly.

8. I didn't get any call from the astrologer for the video consultation?

In case the call gets disconnected in between the consultation, don’t worry, the astrologer will call you back shortly.

8. Where can I find my prediction document after the consultation?

We apologise for this inconvenience. Please write to us at admin@magratechs.com and we will be glad to help you.

9. What is the fees for video consultation?

You can find the fees for video consultation by selecting the appointment slot for your astrologer and visiting the payments page.

10. I have got a payment failed error but my account has been debited

Please wait for some time and your payment status will get updated automatically. If you still face the problem, please write us at admin@magratechs.com and we will be glad to resolve the issue for you.

11. I would like to get a refund of my video consultation fees

Please write us at admin@magratechs.com and we will be glad to help you.

12. Will I get a full refund for the cancellation of my video consultation appointment?

Absolutely! You can cancel the appointment 2 hours before the appointment time and you will get a 100% refund of your consultation fees.

13. What can I use this app for?

Sun Astro App is a complete Astro online consulting app where you can book your consultation appointments, video consult with your astrologer

14. Is my data safe?

We, at Sun Astro, understand that the privacy of data is one of the fundamental human rights. All your personal data and consultations are completely private and confidential. We are compliant with industry standards to ensure your data is securely stored

14. When will I get my consultation fees refund?

We will initiate the refund as soon as the appointment is cancelled by you. Refund processing will take 7-10 working days and you can expect the refund in your account within this time interval
